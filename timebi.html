Description

TimeBi is an application that was born out of the need for a change in lifestyle. There was a large percentage of people who were affected by the excessive use of social networks.

After a long time of analysis and hard work, our team came to the conclusion to create an application that would help us to prevent people from being affected by this. After 3 years in the industry and being able to notice the great changes we have been able to make, it fills us with gratitude and pride to see how what was once seen as a problem is now starting to be solved.
